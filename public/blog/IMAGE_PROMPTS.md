## Image Specifications

### Featured Images (Blog Posts)
- **Dimensions:** 1200x630px (Open Graph standard)
- **Format:** JPG (optimized for web, ~200KB)
- **Aspect Ratio:** 16:9 (1.91:1)
- **Style:** Professional photography, natural lighting, optimistic tone
- **Colors:** Warm, inviting, professional (avoid harsh contrast)
- **Subjects:** Real-looking people and environments (not obviously AI)

### Author Avatars
- **Dimensions:** 400x400px (square)
- **Format:** JPG (optimized for web, ~50KB)
- **Aspect Ratio:** 1:1 (square)
- **Style:** Professional corporate headshot
- **Background:** Solid color (gray, blue, or white)
- **Lighting:** Professional studio or natural window light
- **Expression:** Warm, approachable, professional smile

## Recommended AI Tools

### Best for Photorealism:
1. **Midjourney v6** (Best overall quality, most realistic)
   - Use `--style raw` for photographic look
   - Use `--v 6` for latest version
   - Add `--ar 16:9` for featured images, `--ar 1:1` for avatars

2. **Leonardo.ai** (Good quality, easier to use)
   - Select "Leonardo Diffusion XL" model
   - Use "Photography" preset
   - Adjust guidance scale to 7-10

3. **DALL-E 3** (Most accessible via ChatGPT Plus)
   - Good for professional business imagery
   - Specify "high-quality photograph" in prompt

### Free Alternatives:
- **Unsplash.com** - Search for "restaurant owner", "corporate lunch", "business headshot"
- **Pexels.com** - Free stock photos (similar search terms)
- **Pixabay.com** - Free stock imagery

## Post-Generation Editing

After generating images:

1. **Resize to exact dimensions:**
   - Featured: 1200x630px
   - Avatars: 400x400px

2. **Optimize file size:**
   - Use TinyPNG.com or Squoosh.app
   - Target ~200KB for featured, ~50KB for avatars

3. **Rename files exactly as specified:**
   - commission-free-restaurant-marketing.jpg
   - attract-corporate-customers-restaurant.jpg
   - etc.

4. **Place in correct directories:**
   - Featured images: `/public/blog/`
   - Author avatars: `/public/team/`

## Tips for Best Results

1. **Midjourney:** Add `--style raw` for less artistic, more photographic results
2. **Avoid obvious AI artifacts:** No extra fingers, weird backgrounds, distorted faces
3. **Test multiple variations:** Generate 3-4 versions, pick the best
4. **Check faces carefully:** Avatars should look like real people
5. **Consistency:** Use similar lighting and color tones across all images
6. **Professional tone:** Avoid overly casual or stock-photo-looking images
